Commissions...

  • are created by a Buyer on an Offer.
  • contain initial requirements from the Buyer about what they want.
  • have their own chat room between the Buyer and the Creator of the offer.
Once a Commission is created, it is put into the Review state. The Creator will usually either accept or reject the Commission after looking at the initial requirements and potentially communicating with the buyer. The Creator of the Offer will be notified that a commission has been requested on their Offer.

Here are the different states of a Commission:
State Meaning
{% include "furfolio/commissions/state_badge.html" with state="REVIEW" only %} The Commission needs to be reviewed by the Creator of the Offer.
{% include "furfolio/commissions/state_badge.html" with state="ACCEPTED" only %} The Creator has decided that they are wanting to work on the Commission.
{% include "furfolio/commissions/state_badge.html" with state="IN_PROGRESS" only %} The Creator is currently working on the commission.
{% include "furfolio/commissions/state_badge.html" with state="CLOSED" only %} The Commission is considered done and no more work is needed.
{% include "furfolio/commissions/state_badge.html" with state="REJECTED" only %} The Creator has decided that they are not willing to work on the Commission.
Every Commission is in one state at a time. Between the Buyer and the Creator, only the Creator can change the state of a Commission.

Self-managed Commissions

Self-managed Commissions are a feature that allow Creators to track Commissions that happen outside of Furfolio. This means that Furfolio can track Commissions initiated outside of the Furfolio platform. When a Creator requests a Commission on their own Offer, that Commission is considered self-managed. These types of Commissions are not subject to the Commission limits configured in the Offer.

Self-managed Commissions satisfy cases that Furfolio cannot fully manage:

  • in-person Commission requests (at convention or elsewhere)
  • to accept special Buyer requests regardless of the Offer limits
