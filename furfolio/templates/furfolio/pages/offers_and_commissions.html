The two main objects in Furfolio are Offers and Commissions.

Offers

They look like this:

{% include "furfolio/offers/offer_card.html" with offer=example_offer only %}

A Creator creates and publishes Offers. Offers are the way Creators say that they are open for commissions. A Buyer looks for an Offer that they are interested in, then they try to make a commission on the Offer.

Offers also give Creators the power to limit commissions from buyers.

If you want to learn more about Offers, see the Offer reference.

Commissions

Commissions are created by Buyers on an Offer they found. They represent an ongoing transaction between the Buyer and the Creator. Furfolio helps track the progress of Commissions. A Chat is also provided between the Buyer and Creator.

If you want to learn more about Commissions, see the Commission reference.
