The two main objects in Furfolio are Offers and Commissions.

Offers...

  • are created usually by Creators on the platform.
  • express that a Creator is open to being commissioned as described in the Offer they publish.
  • explain what service is being provided, a price range, and the number of avaliable slots.
  • are time-bound and only allow commissions to be requested before an expiration date.
  • provide functions that regulate the number of commissions.
Offers are either open or closed.
  • Open means that the offer has not expired and the Creator has not forced it closed.
  • Closed means that the offer is not open. Buyers cannot request commissions of closed offers.
An open Offer communicates that it can potentially be commissioned. However, even if an Offer is open, you may not be able to commission it; this could be becuase the Creator has configured it to limit incoming commissions using a special rule.

Commissions...

  • are created from a Buyer on an Offer.
  • contain initial requirements from the Buyer about what they want.
  • have their own chat room between the Buyer and the Creator of the offer.
Once a Commission is created, it is put into the Review state. The Creator will usually either accept or reject the Commission after looking at the initial requirements and potentially communicating with you.

Here are the different states a Commission can assume:
State Meaning
Review The Commission needs to be reviewed by the Creator of the Offer.
Accepted The Creator has decided that they are wanting to work on the Commission.
In Progress The Creator is currently working on the commission.
Finished The Commission is considered done and no more work is needed.
Rejected The Creator has decided that they are not willing to work on the Commission.
Between the Buyer and the Creator, only the Creator can change the state of a Commission.

Self-managed Commissions

Self-managed Commissions are a feature that allow Creators to track Commissions out-of-band of the software. This means that Furfolio can track Commissions initiated outside of the Furfolio platform. When a Creator requests a Commission on their own Offer, that Commission is considered self-managed. These types of Commissions are not subject to the Commission limits configured in the Offer.

Self-managed Commissions satisfy cases that Furfolio cannot fully manage:

  • in-person Commission requests (at convention or elsewhere)
  • to accept special Buyer requests regardless of the Offer limits
  • non-Commission task tracking using a forced-closed Offer
