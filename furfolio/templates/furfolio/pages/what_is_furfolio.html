Furfolio is an online commission management system that helps with the entire commission process.

Why Furfolio?

Image an artist wanting to accept commissions for art they create; they may do the following tasks:

  • Announce they are open for commissions to followers.
  • Open a survey page to accept commissions.
  • Manually manage survey results to prevent being overloaded with requests.
  • Track progress of commissions using different software.
  • Manage channels of communication.
  • Organize commissions and offers from the past and present.
Furfolio handles all of these tasks on one platform.

Who is it for?

Furfolio has two main types of users: Creators and Buyers:

  • Creators: publish offers and work on commissions.
  • Buyers: request commissions of offers.
Tools for Creators
  • Offer publication and notification
  • Commission progress tracking and kanban board
  • Automation to manage incoming commissions
  • Communication channels to Buyers per commission
  • Powerful commission search and filters
  • Tracking commissions outside of Furfolio using self-managed commissions
Tools for Buyers
  • Offer search
  • Progress tracking of commissions
  • Communication to Creator per commission
  • Consent to see NSFW (adult) offers
Furfolio does NOT
  • Handle payment between Buyers and Creators
  • Act as a social media application
