Furfolio is an online commission management system that understands the entire lifetime of the commission process.

Why Furfolio?

Image this scenario: An artist wants to open commissions for drawing portraits of characters. To do this, the artist does the following tasks:

  • Mention they are open for commissions on social media platforms.
  • Open a survey page to accept commissions.
  • Open and publish a task tracker or kanban board to communicate progress to buyers.
  • Establish and manage channels of communication between themselves and buyers.
Besides social media, Furfolio handles all of these scenarios as one application.

Who is it for?

Furfolio has two main types of users: Creators and Buyers:

  • Creators: users who primarily publish offers indicating that they are open for commissions.
  • Buyers: users who primarily request commissions of offers.
Tools for Creators
  • Offer publication
  • Commission progress tracking and kanban board
  • Various automated rules of limiting incoming commissions
  • Communication between themselves and Buyers per commission
  • Granular commission search
  • Support for tracking out-of-band commissions via self-managed commissions
Tools for Buyers
  • Offer search
  • Progress tracking of commissions
  • Communication to Creator per commission
  • Consent to NSFW (adult) offers
Furfolio does NOT
  • Handle payment between Buyers and Creators
  • Act as a social media application
